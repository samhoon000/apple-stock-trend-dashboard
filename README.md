# 📈 Apple Stock Market Analysis Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** built to analyze historical Apple stock data and uncover meaningful financial insights related to price trends, volatility, trading behavior, and market movement patterns.

The goal of this project is to transform raw stock market data into easy-to-understand visual analytics that can help learners, investors, and analysts better understand stock behavior over time.

---

## 🚀 Project Objective

Stock market datasets contain large amounts of numerical data that are difficult to interpret directly.
This project helps simplify that information through visual storytelling and business intelligence techniques.

Using Power BI, the dashboard answers questions such as:

* How has Apple stock grown over time?
* Which months historically performed better?
* How volatile was the stock across different years?
* Does higher trading volume always mean larger price movement?
* How frequently did bullish and bearish trading days occur?

## 📈 Key Insights From Analysis

### 🔹 Long-Term Growth Trend

Apple stock demonstrated strong long-term growth, especially after 2016, indicating increasing market value and investor confidence.

### 🔹 Volatility Increase Near 2020

A significant increase in volatility was observed around 2020, where the stock experienced larger daily price fluctuations.

### 🔹 Monthly Performance Patterns

February and October showed relatively stronger average closing prices compared to several other months.

### 🔹 Bullish vs Bearish Days

Bullish trading days slightly outnumbered bearish days, suggesting an overall positive market trend during the analyzed period.

### 🔹 Trading Volume Observation

Although trading volume gradually decreased over the years, the stock price continued to rise — showing stronger valuation despite reduced average volume.

# 📈 Apple Stock Price Analysis Dashboard

An interactive **Power BI dashboard** project analyzing historical Apple stock market data to uncover trends, volatility patterns, monthly performance, and trading behavior.

This project transforms raw stock data into business-friendly visual insights using data cleaning, modeling, DAX measures, and dashboard storytelling.

---

## 🚀 Project Overview

The dashboard helps users understand:

- Long-term Apple stock growth trend  
- Monthly average closing price behavior  
- Stock volatility across years  
- Relationship between trading volume and price movement  
- Bullish vs Bearish trading day counts  
- High-volume trading dates and price fluctuations

## 📊 Dashboard Features

### 1️⃣ Apple Stock Trend & Monthly Performance

- Closing price trend over multiple years  
- Average closing price by month  
- Maximum stock closing price  
- Minimum stock closing price  

### 2️⃣ Stock Price Behavior & Volatility Insights

- Average daily price range by year  
- Volume vs Daily Range scatter analysis  
- Yearly average trading volume trend  
- Bullish / Bearish / Neutral day classification  
- Detailed trading records table  

---

## 📌 Key Insights

- Apple stock showed strong long-term upward growth.
- Major volatility spike observed near 2020.
- February had one of the highest average monthly closing prices.
- Bullish trading days slightly exceeded bearish days.
- Trading volume reduced over time while price value increased.
